Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes.

How long does a typical painting project take?

The duration of a painting project varies depending on several factors, including the size of the space, the number of rooms, the condition of the surfaces, and the complexity of the project. On average, a single room can take 1-3 days to complete, while a full house may take 5-10 days. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation, taking into account your specific requirements and any special considerations.

Are your cleaning products safe for pets and children?

Absolutely! We understand the importance of a safe environment for your loved ones, including pets. All our cleaning products are non-toxic, biodegradable, and plant-based. They're free from harsh chemicals, artificial fragrances, and dyes that could potentially harm children or pets. We use products that are certified by Green Seal and Safer Choice, ensuring they meet the highest standards for environmental and human safety.

Do you offer color consultation for painting projects?

Yes, we offer comprehensive color consultation as part of our painting services. Our color specialists have extensive knowledge of color theory, trends, and how different shades can impact the mood and aesthetics of a space. We'll help you choose the perfect palette for your project, considering factors like lighting, room size, and your personal preferences. We provide color samples and can even create custom color blends if you have a specific vision in mind.

How do you ensure the quality of your work?

Quality is at the core of everything we do at Fresh Feel. We maintain rigorous quality control standards throughout every project. Our process includes thorough surface preparation, multiple paint coats for optimal coverage, and meticulous attention to detail in every aspect of the job. Each project is supervised by experienced project managers who ensure that all work meets our high standards. We also conduct final inspections before project completion to ensure your complete satisfaction.

What areas do you serve?

Fresh Feel Painting & Cleaning proudly serves the entire Greater Toronto Area, including downtown Toronto, North York, Scarborough, Etobicoke, Mississauga, Brampton, and surrounding areas. We have a fleet of fully equipped vehicles and a team of mobile professionals ready to serve you wherever you are in our service area.
