Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Fresh Feel Painting & Cleaning collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Introduction

Fresh Feel Painting & Cleaning ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, disclosure, and protection of your information when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with us in any way.

This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected through our website, mobile applications, and any other digital platforms we operate, as well as information collected offline when you request or receive our services.

By accessing and using our services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use our services. We encourage you to review this policy regularly, as we may update it from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws.

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide Directly

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address when you request a quote, schedule a service, or contact us
  • Service Information: Details about the services you request, including location, property type, scope of work, and any special requirements
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details, and transaction history (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
  • Communication Data: Messages, inquiries, feedback, and any other correspondence you send to us
  • Account Information: Username, password, and profile details if you create an account on our website

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website or use our services, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and internet service provider
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, links clicked, search queries, and other browsing behavior
  • Location Information: General geographic location based on IP address (not precise GPS location unless you explicitly authorize it)
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: Information collected through cookies, web beacons, pixels, and similar tracking technologies

Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third parties, including:

  • Payment processors and financial institutions
  • Service providers and business partners
  • Publicly available sources and data providers
  • Social media platforms if you connect your account with us
  • Referral sources and marketing partners

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

Service Delivery

Processing your service requests, scheduling appointments, providing quotes, delivering our painting and cleaning services, and managing your account

Communication

Responding to your inquiries, sending service confirmations, providing updates about your projects, and addressing any concerns or complaints

Billing and Payments

Processing payments, generating invoices, managing refunds, and maintaining accurate financial records

Marketing and Promotions

Website Improvement

Analyzing website usage patterns, improving user experience, personalizing content, and optimizing our services

Legal and Security

Complying with legal obligations, enforcing our agreements, protecting against fraud, and maintaining security

Analytics and Research

Conducting research, generating statistical reports, and understanding customer preferences and trends

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us recognize you, remember your preferences, and understand how you use our site. We use both session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which remain on your device for a longer period).

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality and security
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and enhance user experience
  • Marketing Cookies: Track your activity to deliver targeted advertising

Managing Cookies

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent. However, blocking cookies may affect website functionality and your user experience.

We also respect "Do Not Track" signals in your browser. If you enable Do Not Track, we will limit our collection of tracking information, though some features may not function optimally.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, processing payments, scheduling services, and delivering our products and services

Business Partners

We may share information with business partners for joint marketing initiatives, co-branded services, or other collaborative purposes

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our legal rights and interests

Business Transfers

If we merge, are acquired, or sell our business or assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction

Consent

We may share your information with third parties when you explicitly consent to such sharing

Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement appropriate technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Security Measures Include:

  • Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption for data transmission
  • Firewalls and intrusion detection systems
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication protocols
  • Employee training on data protection and privacy practices
  • Secure data storage and backup procedures

While we strive to protect your information, no security system is completely impenetrable. We cannot guarantee absolute security, and you assume all risk associated with providing information to us. Please notify us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access to your account or information.

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

Access

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you

Correction

You can request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information about you

Deletion

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the Contact Us section below. We will respond to your request within the timeframe required by applicable law.

Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete such information promptly.

For children between 13 and 18, we provide limited data collection practices and encourage parents to supervise their children's online activities. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

Our website may contain links to third-party websites and services that are not operated by Fresh Feel Painting & Cleaning. This Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party websites, and we are not responsible for their privacy practices.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites before providing your personal information. Your use of third-party websites is at your own risk and subject to their terms and policies.

International Data Transfers

Fresh Feel Painting & Cleaning operates primarily in Canada. If you are located outside Canada and provide information to us, your information may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in Canada, where data protection laws may differ from your country of residence.

By providing information to us, you consent to the transfer of your information to Canada and its use in accordance with this Privacy Policy. We implement appropriate safeguards to protect your information during international transfers.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services, fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, and comply with legal obligations. The retention period varies depending on the type of information and the purposes for which we use it.

For example:

  • Service and billing information is retained for the duration of our business relationship and as required for tax and accounting purposes
  • Marketing information is retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion
  • Website usage data is typically retained for analytics purposes for up to 24 months
  • Information required for legal compliance is retained as long as legally required

When information is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it. However, we may retain certain information in aggregated or anonymized form for statistical and analytical purposes.

Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Your continued use of our website and services following the posting of changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your information.
